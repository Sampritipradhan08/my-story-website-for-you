Echoes of my Silence

You know, Gobindo, you were my first love… or maybe I shouldn’t say were, because you are still here,
and perhaps you always will be. I don’t know why the universe separated us, but believe me, not a single day has passed that I haven’t thought of you. I love you… maybe I never knew how to show it the way I wanted to.
You know, I think of you so often, and when I do, I look at your pictures. It hurts sometimes,
I know—I was selfish, and that’s why seeing you with someone else feels painful—but I know, someday,
I will get used to it. I tried to forget you, but I couldn’t… so I stopped trying.
As long as I live, I will live with this ache, and somehow, that’s what keeps me going. I miss you so much… something I will never say out loud.
