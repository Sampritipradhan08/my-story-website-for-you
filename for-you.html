A Heart Still Waiting

To you, my dearest,
I know our story is not complete, but I am still waiting.

I don’t know what the future holds, but I want to see you happy — even if it’s not with me.

My heart can’t fully pray for you to be happy with anyone else, but I try anyway.
Every day, I send silent prayers, wishing you joy, peace, and love.

Though our paths may have diverged, my heart remains yours quietly, and I will keep waiting... hoping that life might still bring you back to me one day.
